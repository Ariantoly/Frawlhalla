Frawlcome

Event Duration: May 20 - 28, 2021

Welcome to Frawlsallathe free fighting game that you can enjoy alone or with friends. To welcome new players we will give out some rewards for some lucky players and create some missions that would give out abundant of reward.

Everyday during the duration of this event there will be 7 random lucky players that will be given one free skin free of their choice. To get a chance to be this lucky player, you need to have a minimum of 3 hours of play time that day. Aside from this raffle, everyday during this event period, every players will receive 2x bonus of gold and exp from every game, so don't miss out.

There are also some missions that will give you some interesting rewards, one of which is a skin for Koji. These missions will be unlocked periodically everyday, so keep checking everyday and do the mission to get your free Koji skin at the end of this event.
