Cyber Madness

Event Duration: July 29 - August 7, 2021

Executing Program 64... Hello humans we are cyber, we are many and we are one. We come as we are to eradicate humanity, be prepare and be scared because we will spare no one. Long live Cyber.

Oh no!! the universe is in great danger, quickly heroes let us recruit our legends to fight the upcoming Cyber Invasion. In this event there will be an exclusive gamemode where you will fight against the Cyber army. These cyber army drops gears that can be collected after each game has been completed. Don't worry it doesn't matter if you lose or win, you will still get the gears based on how many Cyber you kill.

These gears can be used to purchase something in the shop like skins, emotes, and many more. Be sure to spend all of them because the gears will dissapear once the event ends. There are a few missions that can give you gears as a reward.

Missions Rewards
Log-in for 3 days in a row 500 Gears
Kill 10 Cybers 100 Gears
Kill 50 Cybers 250 Gears
Kill 100 Cybers 500 Gears
Spend 100 Gears 200 Gears
Spend 500 Gears 1000 Gears
