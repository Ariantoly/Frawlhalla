LEGENDS

Koji

Koji, The Samurai, is a legend that have high dexterity and good speed. Koji wears 2 weapons, a katana and a bow making him bery versatile to attack from close range and also mid range.

Bödvar

Bödvar, The Viking, is a legend that wields a sword and hammer in battle. Bödvar is very strong, having great strength and also dexterity making him very hard to be taken down in the battlefield.

Val

Val, The Weapon, is a legend that is best known for her mobility and her defense. While she is quick, she doesn't have a lot of strength making her attacks a little bit weak. In battle, she wields her ganuntlets and her sword.
