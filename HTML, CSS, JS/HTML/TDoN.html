The Darkest of Nights

Event Duration: July 1 - 9, 2021

Beware of the night that engulfs, the shadow that stares eerily, and the howl of the cold night wind for she has come, she the one that consumes the darkness and lives in the dark.

Meet the newest legend that has been added to Frawlsalla, Petra, The Darkheart. With the power of darkness she will wreak havoc in the battlefield. During this event period we will give all players 3 times to use Petra for free. Aside from free trials, we also will give out new missions that would require you to use Petra once.
